WELCOME TO THE REGISTRATION PAGE

WANT TO JOIN THE TEAM? FOLLOW THESE STEPS USING THE LEFT NAVIGATION.

  1. SIGN IN OR CREATE AN ACCOUNT.
  2. SIGN UP ON IMLEAGUES.

    Sign in with your UMD SSO. Navigate to Club Sports. Select “Join Team” next to Men’s Rugby. Complete your personal form, concussion quiz, and sign the participation form.

  3. PAY YOUR DUES THROUGH PAYPAL.

    Dues are $120.

  4. CONFIRM YOUR SIGNUP AND WAIT FOR APPROVAL.
  5. CHECK YOUR STATUS.
